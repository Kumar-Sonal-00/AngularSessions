The tiger (Panthera tigris) is a member of the genus Panthera
and the largest living cat species native to Asia.
It has a powerful, muscular body with a large head and paws,
a long tail and orange fur with black, mostly vertical stripes. It is traditionally classified into nine recent subspecies, though some recognise only two subspecies, mainland Asian tigers
